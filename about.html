About Me

I’ve spent my life questioning the world—from science to religion, philosophy to psychology, spirituality to everyday life—and through it all, I’ve been searching for truth, meaning, and freedom. My journey has taken me from exploring the divine, from studying religion and science to understanding human nature, and from overthinking life’s complexities to embracing simplicity and joy.

I’ve learned that life isn’t about fear, rules, or fitting into boxes—it’s about experiencing, learning, and growing. I journal daily, reflect deeply, and try to live with empathy, love, and awareness. I believe in being true to oneself, finding balance between solitude and connection, and valuing health, relationships, and freedom over material wealth.

This space is where I share my reflections, insights, and experiences—about spirituality, philosophy, psychology, and the little moments that make life meaningful. I hope my journey inspires others to question, explore, and live fully without fear.
